About A Tad of Pizzazz

Welcome to a world where creativity knows no bounds and every visit feels like discovering a hidden treasure chest. A Tad of Pizzazz is more than just a boutique; it's a celebration of the whimsical, the unique, and the beautifully crafted.

A Tad of Pizzazz has been a familiar face at makers markets throughout Rhode Island, Massachusetts, and Connecticut for several years. Enthusiastic responses to the unique baby items and fascinators were always encouraging. The most frequent question was, "Where can we find your shop?" Our brick-and-mortar store officially opened in May 2025 at 65 Newport Avenue in Rumford, RI. Come experience the full range of handcrafted treasures!

The boutique goes beyond a simple store by filling its space with unique, handmade, and themed items that spark the imagination and delight the senses. With thoughtful presentations and playful gifts, like creative baby items from The Confaketionary and stylish hair accessories from Hair Flair, the shop aims to be an experience of joy and discovery.

Every visit to our boutique is an adventure where carefully curated treasures await around every corner. From luxurious bath products to whimsical home decor, we believe in the power of small delights to brighten your day.
